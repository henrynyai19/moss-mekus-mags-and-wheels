Our mandate

Building a fair, open, and ambitious marketplace.

The Ministry Of Commerce coordinates policies and public services that help enterprises trade with confidence, investors navigate opportunity, and communities benefit from inclusive growth.

We focus on practical market access, digital trade readiness, consumer protection, export development, and partnerships that turn local capability into regional and global competitiveness.
